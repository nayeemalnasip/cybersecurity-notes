# Cyber Security Fundamentals — Study Note

## Conclusion: The CIA Triad and the Cyber Security Mindset

### Overview

This room introduced the fundamental objective of cyber security: protecting digital information and services from threats that could compromise their security.

The core framework used throughout cybersecurity is the **CIA Triad**, which consists of:

```text
Confidentiality
Integrity
Availability
```

These three principles define what security professionals aim to protect and serve as the foundation for nearly every security control, policy, assessment, and incident response process.

---

## Main Concept

### What is the CIA Triad?

The CIA Triad is a security model used to evaluate and protect digital assets.

It focuses on three essential objectives:

| Principle       | Purpose                                           |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Confidentiality | Prevent unauthorized access to information        |
| Integrity       | Prevent unauthorized modification of information  |
| Availability    | Ensure information and services remain accessible |

Every cybersecurity activity ultimately supports one or more of these objectives.

---

## How It Works

### Confidentiality

Protects sensitive information from being viewed by unauthorized individuals.

Examples:

```text
Passwords
Medical Records
Financial Information
Business Documents
```

Common Security Controls:

```text
Encryption
Authentication
Access Control
Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
```

---

### Integrity

Ensures information remains accurate, complete, and trustworthy.

Examples:

```text
Bank Transactions
Student Grades
Audit Logs
Business Records
```

Common Security Controls:

```text
Hashing
Digital Signatures
Checksums
Version Control
```

---

### Availability

Ensures systems, services, and data remain accessible when needed.

Examples:

```text
Websites
Online Banking
Email Systems
Cloud Services
```

Common Security Controls:

```text
Backups
Redundant Infrastructure
Load Balancing
Disaster Recovery Plans
```

---

## Important Components

### Confidentiality

Definition:

```text
Ensuring digital information is not available
to unauthorized individuals.
```

Objective:

```text
Protect Privacy
Protect Sensitive Data
Prevent Information Disclosure
```

---

### Integrity

Definition:

```text
Ensuring digital information is not modified
without permission.
```

Objective:

```text
Maintain Accuracy
Prevent Data Tampering
Preserve Trustworthiness
```

---

### Availability

Definition:

```text
Ensuring digital information is available
when needed by authorized users.
```

Objective:

```text
Maintain Service Access
Prevent Downtime
Support Business Operations
```

---

### Security Mindset

Cyber security professionals use the CIA Triad to evaluate incidents and determine impact.

Typical questions include:

```text
Was information exposed?
Was information altered?
Was access disrupted?
```

These questions help classify security incidents and guide response actions.
